1. About This App

DeFi Analytics is a personal portfolio tracker for decentralized finance (DeFi) investors. It is a free, open web application available at yuriilosiev-png.github.io/defi-app/ and as an Android APK.

The app helps users monitor their DeFi positions across multiple protocols and blockchains, including Aave, Uniswap, GMX, Morpho, Compound, Merkl, and Solana.

2. Data We Collect

DeFi Analytics collects no personal data. The app does not require registration and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

What you enter into the app

The only input the app uses is your public blockchain wallet address (e.g. 0x... for EVM chains or a base58 address for Solana). This address is:

  • Stored locally on your device using browser localStorage
  • Used only to query public blockchain APIs
  • Never transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)
  • Never sold or shared with third parties
Journal and settings data

Any data you manually enter (journal entries, pool settings, asset allocations) is stored exclusively on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Public Blockchain Data

To display your DeFi positions, the app queries the following public APIs using your wallet address:

  • Ankr API — multichain EVM token balances and Aave positions
  • Helius API — Solana native balance and SPL token balances
  • DefiLlama — DeFi protocol positions across chains
  • Merkl API — accumulated DeFi rewards
  • Morpho GraphQL — Morpho Blue lending positions
  • GMX Subgraph — GMX GLV pool balances
  • Blockscout — EVM fallback when Ankr is unavailable
  • Binance / CoinGecko / CryptoCompare — asset price feeds

All blockchain data queried is public information — the same data anyone can view on a block explorer by entering your address. No private or sensitive information is accessed.

4. Telegram Integration (Optional)

The app includes an optional Telegram notification feature via the @DefiAnalytics2bot bot. If you choose to use this feature:

  • You provide your Telegram username to connect the bot
  • The bot sends alerts to your Telegram account (Health Factor drops, price levels, LP range exits)
  • Your Telegram username is stored locally on your device only
  • The bot operates via the Render.com cloud service solely to relay alert messages
  • This feature is entirely optional and can be skipped

5. Cookies and Tracking

DeFi Analytics does not use cookies, does not use analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.), and does not contain any advertising or third-party tracking scripts.

Data persistence is achieved solely through browser localStorage, which is local to your device and not accessible by any server.

6. Children's Privacy

DeFi Analytics is intended for adults (18+) interested in decentralized finance. The app does not knowingly collect data from or target children under the age of 18.

7. Security

Since the app stores all data locally and transmits only public wallet addresses to public APIs, the security risk is minimal. However, users should be aware:

  • Never enter your private key or seed phrase anywhere in this app — it is not required and should never be shared
  • The app operates in watch-only (read-only) mode — it cannot sign transactions or move your funds
  • Price and position data is sourced from third-party APIs and may occasionally be inaccurate

8. Disclaimer

DeFi Analytics is an informational tool only. It does not provide financial, investment, or legal advice. All data displayed is for informational purposes. Always verify important financial decisions through official protocol interfaces and consult a qualified financial advisor if needed.
